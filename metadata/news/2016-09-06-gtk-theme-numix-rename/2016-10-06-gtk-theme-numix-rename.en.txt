x11-themes/gtk-theme-numix was renamed as x11-themes/numix-gtk-theme

gtk-theme-numix package was renamed to follow upstream repository name.
Please uninstall the old package and install the new one instead:

    cave resolve \!x11-themes/gtk-theme-numix x11-themes/numix-gtk-theme
